Connect Your Canvas

Paste your Canvas API token to see your courses and generate briefs for any session.

How do I get my token?
  1. Go to canvas.stanford.edu
  2. Click your profile icon (top-left) then Settings
  3. Scroll to Approved Integrations
  4. Click + New Access Token
  5. Give it a name (e.g. "Canvas Brief") and click Generate Token
  6. Copy the token and paste it above

Your token is used only during your session and is never stored.
